# Game Thumbnail Guide

## Quick Options:

### 1. Take In-Game Screenshot (BEST)
1. In Roblox Studio, press F5 to test
2. Position camera for best view of your city
3. Press Print Screen
4. Open Paint, paste (Ctrl+V)
5. Crop to 1920x1080
6. Save as PNG

### 2. Use Canva (FREE)
1. Go to canva.com
2. Search "Roblox Game Thumbnail"
3. Use template, add text: "BloxVille"
4. Download as PNG (1920x1080)

### 3. Studio Screenshot
1. In Studio, go to View tab
2. Click "Game Settings"
3. Click "Places" 
4. Under Icon, click "Choose File"
5. Take screenshot first, then upload

## Recommended Size:
- 1920x1080 (16:9 ratio)
- PNG format
- Under 5MB

## What to Show:
- Your city buildings
- Character in center
- Text: "BloxVille - Open World City"
- Bright, colorful, exciting
