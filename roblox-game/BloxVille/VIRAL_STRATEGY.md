- Time-gated content

### 2. Social Proof
- Leaderboards
- Achievement showcases

- Milestone celebrations

### 3. Network Effects

### 4. Mystery & Discovery

### 5. Progression Loops

- Rebirth system

## Monetization Strategy

### Free-to-Play Friendly
- No pay-to-win
- All content accessible for free
- Grind vs Pay option
- Generous free rewards

### VIP Value Proposition
- 2x cash (saves time)
- Exclusive vehicles (cosmetic flex)
- Bigger houses (show off)
- Early access to updates

### Developer Products
- Small packs ($100 = 99 Robux) - impulse buy
- Medium packs ($500 = 299 Robux) - best value
- Large packs ($2500 = 999 Robux) - whales

### Limited Offers
- Flash sales (50% off, 2 hours only)
- Bundle deals (VIP + Cash = discount)
- Seasonal packs (Christmas bundle)

## Emergency Plans

### If Launch Flops (< 100 CCU Day 1)
1. Increase ad budget 5x
2. Give away free VIP passes
3. Host live event with giveaways
4. Reach out to more influencers
5. Analyze what went wrong

### If Game Breaks
1. Shutdown servers immediately
2. Post update on social media
3. Fix critical bugs
4. Compensate players (free cash)
5. Re-launch with apology

### If Negative Reviews
1. Respond to every review
2. Fix mentioned issues ASAP
3. Post update changelog
4. Ask satisfied players to review
