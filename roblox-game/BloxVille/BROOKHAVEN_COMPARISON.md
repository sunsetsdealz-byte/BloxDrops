## Risk Assessment

### What Could Go Wrong:

**1. Brookhaven copies features**
- Unlikely (they're established, slow to update)
- We can iterate faster
- Our unique angle (pizza focus) is defensible

**2. Not enough marketing budget**
- Start with 5K Robux
- Reinvest earnings
- Organic growth through quality

**3. Technical issues**
- Thoroughly test before launch
- Have dev team ready for hotfixes
- Monitor errors in real-time

**4. Low initial traction**
- Expected - all games start small
- Focus on retention over acquisition
- Word of mouth from quality

### Mitigation:

✅ **Quality over quantity** - Better to have 100 happy players than 1000 confused ones
✅ **Fast iteration** - Fix bugs within hours, not days
✅ **Community focus** - Listen to feedback, implement requests
✅ **Long-term mindset** - Success takes 3-6 months, not 3 days
